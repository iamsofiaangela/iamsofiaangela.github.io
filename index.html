Lead-to-Client Pipeline (GoHighLevel)

Challenge: The client needed a structured way to capture leads from their website funnel, nurture them, and ensure they didn’t get lost.

Solution: Built an automated pipeline in GHL with consultation forms, tags, inactivity triggers, and auto-move to sales pipeline.

Result: Improved lead tracking, higher engagement with auto-reminders, and seamless marketing → sales handoff.

Quality Check Automation

Challenge: Operators spent excessive time verifying emails, appointments, and client information manually.

Solution: Designed SOP with auto-entry/manual-entry icons, automating confirmations, scheduling, and feedback form tracking.

Result: Reduced workload by 30–40%, faster QA, and standardized process across teams.

Lead-to-Client Pipeline (GoHighLevel)

Challenge: The client needed a structured way to capture and nurture leads.

Solution: Automated pipeline in GHL with forms, tags, inactivity triggers, and auto-move to Sales pipeline.

Result: Improved lead tracking, higher engagement, and seamless handoff from marketing → sales.

Tools Used: GoHighLevel, Google Calendar, Email Automation

Quality Check Automation

Challenge: Operators spent excessive time verifying emails and client data manually.

Solution: SOP with auto-entry/manual-entry, automated confirmations, scheduling, and feedback tracking.

Result: Workload reduced by 30–40%, faster QA, standardized processes.

Tools Used: GoHighLevel, SOP Docs, Email, Google Calendar

Checkout Flow Heuristic Review

Challenge: Billing & onboarding process had friction points.

Solution: Reviewed flow: contract signing triggered welcome email, client code, finance notifications, billing prep.

Result: Faster billing, improved UX, fewer drop-offs.

Tools Used: Heuristic QA, GoHighLevel, Email Automation

Integration of GHL to QuickBooks via Pabbly

Challenge: Finance team struggled with manual invoice creation.

Solution: Pabbly automated invoice creation in QuickBooks from GHL stage “Prepared Billing.”

Result: Eliminated duplicate entry, fewer errors, real-time visibility.

Tools Used: GoHighLevel, Pabbly, QuickBooks, ClickUp
